A Different Direction

There was a time where I put down technology to see what I could find in literature and philosophy. Diving Into Research

In the summer before the final year of my undergraduate studies in SIAT, I earned a grant from NSERC to do interaction design research for one of my professors. It started simply as a curiosity—something new and unusual but also more focused and more challenging—and turned into a trajectory.

That one summer project turned into a year-long investigation into location-based storytelling that produced a fully functional web application, a pair of conference paper submissions, and further funding.
